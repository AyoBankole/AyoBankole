About Me

From Marine Depths to Digital Frontiers

My journey into technology is one of passion-driven curiosity. It began with a Bachelor's degree in Marine Biology from the University of Lagos, where I achieved a First-Class honors and developed a strong foundation in analytical thinking and complex systems. This scientific background instilled in me a desire to solve intricate problems, a passion I soon discovered was perfectly suited for the world of data and Artificial Intelligence.

Transitioning into tech, I immersed myself in data science and machine learning, driven by a mission to create impactful solutions. I've had the privilege of working with organizations like SuperProf and participating in the prestigious 3MTT Fellowship by Nigeria's Federal Government. My work with The 234 Project Community as an AI Engineering Fellow has allowed me to lead projects from concept to prototype, including the development of VisoVox, an AI accessibility tool.

Community is at the heart of what I do. I find immense joy in mentoring and have guided students in deploying machine learning models, fostering the next wave of tech talent. Whether it's through developing AI-powered healthcare solutions or automating business intelligence, my goal remains the same: to use technology to build a better, more accessible world.
