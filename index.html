About Me

I'm a passionate Machine Learning Engineer and AI Developer with a deep fascination for creating intelligent systems that make a meaningful impact. My journey into AI began with a simple question: "How can we use data to solve problems that matter?"

Today, I specialize in building accessible, impact-driven AI tools that bridge the gap between complex technology and real-world applications. From healthcare AI assistants to customer analytics platforms, I focus on creating solutions that are not just technically sound, but genuinely useful for people and businesses.

Beyond my technical work, I serve as a Volunteer Manager at MushinToTheWorld Foundation, where I've had the privilege of training over 25 people in data science and AI fundamentals. There's something incredibly rewarding about watching someone's eyes light up when they grasp a complex concept or build their first machine learning model.

I believe in the power of community and knowledge sharing. Whether it's through mentoring, open-source contributions, or speaking at local tech meetups, I'm always looking for ways to make AI more accessible and understandable for everyone.
